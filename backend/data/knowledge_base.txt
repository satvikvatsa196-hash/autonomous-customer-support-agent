# Refund Policy
Refunds are strictly processed within 14 days of the original purchase date. 
To be eligible for a refund, the item must be unused, in its original packaging, and accompanied by a receipt or proof of purchase.
Refunds will be issued to the original method of payment. Shipping costs are non-refundable.

# Return Policy
You have 30 calendar days to return an item from the date you received it.
Returns must be shipped to our central warehouse. 
Custom-made products, perishable goods, and digital downloads cannot be returned.
If the item is damaged upon arrival, please contact support within 48 hours for a free replacement.

# Shipping Policy
We offer standard shipping (5-7 business days) and expedited shipping (2-3 business days).
Orders are processed within 24 hours. Weekend orders are processed on the next business day.
Free standard shipping is available for all orders over $50 within the continental US.
International shipping is available, but delivery times and customs fees vary by country.

# FAQ
Q: Can I change my order after placing it?
A: You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it by contacting support. After 1 hour, the order is locked for processing.

Q: How do I track my order?
A: Once your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can also use our chatbot to check your shipping status.

Q: What do I do if I received the wrong item?
A: Please raise a support ticket immediately. We will cover the return shipping costs and send you the correct item.
